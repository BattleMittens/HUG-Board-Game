"Super Epic Gamer Time"

The game is similar to the review game in class but it is revamped. The game's objective is still to own the most squares on the map, but it is much faster paced and more aggressive.

You get dots (units) every turn based off how much land you control, but will get more if you answer the question correctly.

When you answer a question, a green flash afterwards means you are correct, a red flash means you are incorrect.

To take over an enemy's territory, you must kill all the dots in that square then answer a question correctly to take their square.

If you take someone's capitol (the gold square), they lose all their territory and are eliminated from the game.

To place units in a square, left click a square and dots will be added to it.

You can only move dots to a square in or directly next to your territory.

Dots CANNOT be moved once placed, so choose wisely. Scroll around using WASD or the arrow keys.

Once you are done with your turn, hit the high-res arrow in the bottom right.

A resource deposit (the green thing in the middle) gives you +5 extra dots per turn if you own it's tile no matter what.

By taking away a space from an enemy and gaining your own you become much closer to DOMINATION!
